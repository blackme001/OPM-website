The OPM Foundation

Demonstrating the love of Christ through practical, life-changing charity and empowerment programs across the globe.

Education • Primary & Secondary

OPM Free Schools

Operating over 22 completely free schools. Providing qualitative education, free uniforms, books, and meals for the less privileged—not limited to church members.
Healthcare • Maternal Care

OPM Free Specialist Hospital

Offering 100% free comprehensive healthcare, prioritizing maternal and child health to save lives without any form of discrimination.
Housing • Shelter & Rehab

OPM Free Estates & Rehab Centers

Providing entirely free housing and shelter to widows, the homeless, and offering rehabilitation for former commercial sex workers to help them start afresh.
Empowerment • Vocational Training

OPM Free Multi-Skill Center

Empowering youths and inmates with practical, high-income skills such as pipeline welding, ICT, and tailoring to build sustainable futures.
Education • Global Learning

International Scholarships

Sending thousands of less privileged students to study in countries like the Benin Republic and across the globe, fully funded by the church.
