Women's Ministry

Building virtuous women, strong families, and a Godly society through faith and fellowship.

Empowering the Virtuous Woman

The OPM Women's Ministry is a vibrant community dedicated to the spiritual, emotional, and social growth of women. We believe that a woman at the center of God's will is a powerful force for transformation in her home and the world.

Our programs focus on spiritual depth, marital success, parenting wisdom, and financial independence through various empowerment initiatives.

Ministry Focus

  • ❤ Marriage & Family Counseling
  • ❤ Skills Acquisition & Business
